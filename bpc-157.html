Safety & Side Effects

Common Side Effects (Rare & Mild)

BPC-157 is generally well-tolerated. Some patients may experience:

  • Mild injection site redness or irritation
  • Temporary fatigue (typically resolves within days)
  • Slight nausea (rare, usually with higher doses)

Who Should Not Take BPC-157

BPC-157 may not be appropriate if you:

  • Are pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Have active cancer (discuss with your oncologist first)
  • Have known peptide allergies
  • Are under 18 years old

Note: Your physician will review your complete medical history to ensure BPC-157 is safe and appropriate for you.
