FAQs

What is NAD+ and why is it important for recovery?

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a coenzyme found in every cell of your body that's essential for energy production, DNA repair, and cellular health. NAD+ levels naturally decline with age, stress, and illness, leading to fatigue, brain fog, and slower recovery. NAD+ therapy replenishes these levels, supporting mitochondrial function, enhancing cellular repair, and boosting energy at the cellular level. It's particularly beneficial for recovery from physical exertion, stress, and age-related decline.

How does Glutathione support detoxification?

Glutathione is your body's master antioxidant, produced naturally in your liver. It plays a crucial role in neutralizing free radicals, supporting immune function, and detoxifying harmful substances. Glutathione binds to toxins, heavy metals, and oxidative stress byproducts, making them water-soluble so your body can eliminate them. Supplementation through IV therapy or injections ensures optimal levels, supporting liver function, reducing oxidative stress, improving skin health, and enhancing overall cellular protection.

What are the benefits of B-12 injections versus oral supplements?

B-12 injections offer significant advantages over oral supplements:

Higher Bioavailability: Injections bypass digestive system absorption issues, delivering 100% of the dose directly into your bloodstream.

Faster Results: Effects are typically felt within 24-48 hours versus days or weeks with oral supplements.

Better for Absorption Issues: Ideal for people with digestive disorders, low stomach acid, or genetic absorption issues.

Longer Lasting: A single injection can maintain elevated B-12 levels for weeks. B-12 supports energy production, nervous system function, red blood cell formation, and mental clarity.

How quickly will I notice results from recovery treatments?

Results vary by treatment and individual, but typical timelines are:

B-12 Injections: Increased energy within 24-72 hours, sustained benefits for 1-4 weeks.

NAD+ Therapy: Many notice mental clarity and energy improvements within hours to days, with cumulative benefits over 2-4 weeks.

Glutathione: Some feel detoxification effects immediately, skin and immune benefits emerge over 2-4 weeks.

Methylene Blue: Cognitive effects within hours, sustained benefits with regular use.

Optimal results typically require a treatment series rather than single doses.

Are these treatments safe? Are there side effects?

Recovery therapies like NAD+, Glutathione, and B-12 have excellent safety profiles when administered by licensed medical professionals. These are naturally occurring substances in your body, and supplementation is generally well-tolerated.

Possible mild side effects:
NAD+: Temporary flushing, nausea during infusion (usually resolves with slower administration)
Glutathione: Rare minor stomach discomfort
B-12: Extremely rare reactions, virtually no side effects

Your Evolife provider will review your medical history and monitor your response to ensure safety and optimal results.

How often should I receive these treatments?

Treatment frequency depends on your specific goals and which therapies you're using:

NAD+ IV Therapy: Weekly for initial loading phase (4-6 weeks), then bi-weekly or monthly for maintenance

Glutathione: Weekly to bi-weekly for active treatment, monthly for maintenance

B-12 Injections: Weekly to bi-weekly, depending on deficiency severity and response

Methylene Blue: Typically weekly or as prescribed

Your provider will create a personalized protocol based on your biomarkers, symptoms, and goals, adjusting frequency as needed.
