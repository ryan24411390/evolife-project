9. **Secondary CTA** - "Ready to feel like you again?"
10. **Social Proof** - "Join the growing community investing in longevity"
11. **FAQ** (implied from structure)
12. **Additional sections** (pricing, comparisons, etc.)

**Insight:** Eden creates a **complete patient journey** from awareness â†’ education â†’ trust â†’ action

#### C. Design System

**Colors:**
- Primary Dark: `rgb(17, 20, 19)` - Deep charcoal (sophisticated, medical authority)
- Primary Light: `rgb(239, 239, 239)` - Soft grey (clean, approachable)

#### E. Trust Signals

- **Testimonials/Reviews:** 56 elements (heavy social proof)

## 7. CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION INSIGHTS

### What Top Competitors Do Right:

#### A. Progressive Trust Building
1. **Start with benefits** (what's in it for me?)
2. **Show the science** (why does it work?)
3. **Display social proof** (who else uses it?)
4. **Explain the process** (how easy is it?)
5. **Address safety** (is it safe for me?)
6. **Final conversion push** (why now?)

#### B. CTA Strategy
- **Eden:** "See if you qualify" (removes barrier, non-committal)
- **Hims:** "Get started" + "Do I have low T?" (quiz-based engagement)
- **Best Practice:** Use **qualifier language** instead of "buy" language
  - âœ… "See if you qualify"
  - âœ… "Check eligibility"
  - âœ… "Take assessment"
  - âŒ "Buy now"
  - âŒ "Purchase"

#### C. Visual Hierarchy
- **Large, bold headlines** (48-62px)
- **Generous whitespace** (breathing room)
- **Consistent spacing** (8px or 16px grid system)
- **Clear section breaks** (background color changes)
- **Directional cues** (arrows, icons guiding eye)

#### D. Mobile-First Design
- Eden uses **140 images** but page loads fast (optimization)
- Hims has **67 CTAs** but doesn't feel cluttered (spacing)
- Both prioritize **mobile experience** first
