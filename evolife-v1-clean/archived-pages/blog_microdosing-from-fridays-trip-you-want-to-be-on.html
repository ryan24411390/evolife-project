Frequently Asked Questions

What is Microdosing?

Microdosing is the practice of taking very small, sub-perceptual doses of psychedelics or medications like GLP-1s. These doses are intended to deliver benefits like mood enhancement or metabolic support without causing intense effects.



Is Microdosing Safe?

While some people report benefits, research on the safety and long-term effects of microdosing is still limited. Always consult a medical professional.



Is Microdosing GLP-1s Right for You?

Microdosing GLP-1s is an emerging approach that offers a more gradual, potentially gentler introduction to weight-loss medications like semaglutide or tirzepatide. While more research is still needed, many patients find that starting with lower doses helps reduce side effects and improve long-term tolerance.
